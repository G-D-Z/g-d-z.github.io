Operation Ghost, an immersive DayZ server on Xbox where survival is crucial. In this post-apocalyptic world, a powerful group called the One World Order has taken control. They demand strict obedience from all who enter their domain. Follow their rules or face severe consequences.

Surviving in this server is a tough challenge. You need to find resources, make alliances, and engage in combat to stay alive. But be careful because The Ghost is always there, watching your every move. If you make a mistake, you could lose everything.
